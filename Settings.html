Preset Color Themes (click to open/close)



    • Default

      A nice touch of blue and dark blue to make a good looking theme! Set as default as blue is preferred by most people.


    • Forest

      A cool shady forest theme for green color lovers! Mixed together to blend as forest colors! Customize it as you like.


    • Mist

      A purplish theme consisting of purple and a little dark blue to make it a misty color! You may like this theme if you like dark colors!


    • Dull Yellow

      A dull theme consisting of not so bright or dark yellowish colors.


  • Underworld

    Very red! Very bright colors consisting of hot red, dark red and more!!
